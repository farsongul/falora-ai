Everything you're wondering.

Not a thing. Falora.AI walks you through the ritual step by step — whether it's your first time or your hundredth. All you need is curiosity (and ideally, a cup of coffee).

Turkish coffee fortune-telling — tasseography — has been practiced for centuries across the Middle East, the Balkans, and Central Asia. Falora.AI is built with that tradition in mind. The AI doesn't make things up randomly; it draws on symbolic interpretation that has real cultural and historical roots.

After your reading, you'll receive a unique AI-generated visual — an image built from the symbols and themes in your fortune. You can download it immediately and print it yourself, or order a physical print through us. No two are the same.

Both are real experiences. Solo readings tend to be more introspective — people use them for reflection, journaling prompts, or a quiet ritual moment. Group sessions are social and theatrical — the shared element of waiting for each other's fortunes creates genuine conversation. Try solo first, then bring friends.
